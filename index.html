Monday — Upper A
Pull focus · 90 min
🔥 Warm-up 10 min
Cardio + dynamic stretch10 min
5 min bike/treadmill, then arm circles, band pull-aparts, shoulder rolls
Easy pace — get blood moving, not sweating hard
▼
↑ Pull 35 min
Kneeling lat pulldownpull-up5 × 10
Kneel facing cable, pull bar to upper chest, core braced
Rest 90 sec · First exercise — do it fresh. Priority movement.
▼
Deadhangpull-up3 × max
Full hang from bar, shoulders packed down, hold as long as possible
Rest 90 sec · Log your time each session — beat it next week
▼
Seated cable row3 × 10
Pull to belly button, squeeze shoulder blades at the end
Rest 90 sec · Think "elbows to pockets"
▼
Dumbbell bicep curl3 × 12
Alternating, supinate wrist at top, no swinging
Rest 60 sec
▼
↑ Push 25 min
Dumbbell bench press3 × 10
Flat bench, lower to chest, control the descent
Rest 90 sec · Dumbbells are safer than barbell for beginners
▼
Dumbbell shoulder press3 × 10
Seated, press overhead, don't arch lower back
Rest 90 sec
▼
Cable tricep pushdown3 × 12
Rope attachment, elbows tucked, full extension
Rest 60 sec
▼
◈ Core 10 min
Side plankpull-up3 × 30 sec
Elbow under shoulder, hips lifted, straight line — both sides
Rest 45 sec · Lateral stability transfers to hanging control
▼
Long lever PPT plankpull-up3 × 20–30s
Arms extended forward, tuck pelvis to flatten lower back
Rest 45 sec · Hardest plank — builds the hollow body position needed for pull-ups
▼
❄ Cool-down 10 min
Lats, chest, shoulder stretch10 min
Hold each 30 sec — focus on lats and biceps after pull work
Don't skip this — flexibility improves when muscles are warm
